About Me

I am Martin Bentley Krebs, owner and creative director of Windy Meadow Consulting, my freelance graphic design business. I have a number of freelance clients, mostly nonprofits, for whom I provide effective and affordable graphic design services. These projects include:

  • Logo Design/Corporate Identity Programs
  • Publication and Collateral Design
  • Web Development
  • Event Planning Services

During the day, I work for Able-Services, Inc., a non-profit agency that provides licensed day program services to adults with intellectual disabilities, serving as a Program Specialist. After more than 35 years in the advertising and marketing field, I made a career switch to Human Services, specifically working with the adult special needs population, and I haven't looked back since.
